INFJ Basics

INFJs are often called “The Advocate” or “The Mystic.” They are deeply intuitive, emotional, visionary, empathetic, and meaning-driven individuals.

INFJs often feel different from others because they:

  • Think deeply about life
  • Need emotional authenticity
  • See hidden meanings and patterns
  • Feel emotions intensely
  • Value purpose over status
  • Crave deep connection instead of surface interaction
